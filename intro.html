Parkinson Disease Detection

Welcome to Parkinson Disease Detection System

Parkinson's disease is a condition that affects the nervous system, causing challenges like tremors, stiffness, and slowed movement. It is more common in older adults and progresses gradually over time.

While the diagnosis might feel overwhelming, remember that you are not alone. Many people with Parkinson's live full, active lives with proper care and support. Advances in technology, like our system, make it easier to detect and manage the condition early.

Early detection is key to managing symptoms effectively. With our advanced tools, we aim to provide a stress-free experience, helping you focus on the path forward with confidence.
